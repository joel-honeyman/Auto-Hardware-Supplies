// FAQ

Common
Questions

Can't find an answer? Reach out to us directly on Facebook and we'll get back to you quickly.

Ask on Facebook →
Do you stock parts for my specific vehicle? +
We stock parts for most major makes and models. If you're unsure, message us on Facebook with your vehicle year, make, and model and we'll confirm availability right away.
How do I check current stock and pricing? +
The best way is to visit our Facebook page where we post regular stock updates, photos, and prices. You can also send us a direct message for a quick quote on any specific item.
Can I get delivery or do I need to collect? +
We offer both collection and delivery options. Contact us via Facebook to discuss what works best for your situation and location.
Are the parts genuine and quality assured? +
Absolutely. We only source from reputable suppliers and trusted brands. Quality and reliability is at the core of what we do — we wouldn't sell anything we wouldn't use ourselves.
Do you cater to trade customers and workshops? +
Yes! We serve both individual customers and trade/workshop accounts. If you have regular ordering needs, get in touch to discuss trade pricing and arrangements.
What if I'm not sure which part I need? +
Just describe the problem or what you're working on and our team will help identify the right part. We'd rather spend a moment helping you get the right item than have you come back with the wrong one.
